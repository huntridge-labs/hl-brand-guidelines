Brand core

Our north star

Enabling government to leverage emerging technology and to drive beneficial outcomes for the public.

Who we are

We are a Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB) that specializes in helping organizations adopt emerging technology at scale — such as AI, machine learning, and hyper-automation.

We work in small, autonomous teams of elite engineers who solve real problems quickly, fearlessly, and without bureaucracy. We deliver outcomes, not headcount, and we do it with the speed and creativity of a startup and the rigor of a trusted federal partner.

Brand positioning

Huntridge Labs is a veteran-owned innovation crew delivering fast, fearless, outcome-driven AI and automation solutions for federal agencies and prime contractors. We cut through noise, refine requirements quickly, and bring deep technical expertise that moves missions forward from day one.

Brand core

Our 8 brand values

These aren't rules to memorize — they're the principles that guide every decision, every interaction, and every line of work we ship.

01

Mission over margin

Our mission takes precedence over financial gain. We measure success by the positive difference we make, not just the bottom line.

02

Extreme ownership

We take complete responsibility for our actions and outcomes. No excuses — we own our successes and failures alike.

03

Empower your teammates

We foster an environment where every team member feels valued and capable. When we lift each other up, we all rise together.

04

Radical transparency

We share information openly — internally and externally. Transparency builds the trust and accountability that drives better decisions.

05

Be logical & pragmatic

We approach challenges with a clear, rational mindset grounded in evidence. We choose the most sensible course of action, not the most convenient one.

06

Commit to the bit

We go all-in on our identity, vision, and mission. Whatever we take on, we pursue it with full dedication and enthusiasm.

07

Be kind & respectful

We treat every individual with dignity and empathy. Genuine connections and mutual respect are the foundation of everything we build.

08

Outdo yourself

We constantly push beyond our own limits. Excellence isn't a destination — it's a habit of continuously surpassing what we did yesterday.

Brand identity

Logo

The Huntridge Labs mark is an abstracted North Star set within a ship element — a nod to our maritime spirit and our role as navigators of complex federal missions. Precise, minimal, and built to scale.

Logo versions

Five lockups cover all use cases. The primary horizontal lockup is the default — use other versions only when layout demands it.

Brand mark

Standalone mark. Use for favicons, app icons, and contexts too small for the full lockup.

Logotype

Wordmark only. Use when the mark has already been established in context.

Primary horizontal lockup

Default logo. Use this in most applications — headers, presentations, documents.

Secondary horizontal lockup

Alternate horizontal arrangement. Use when the primary horizontal doesn't fit the layout.

Vertical lockup

Stacked arrangement for square or portrait contexts — social profiles, print collateral.

Color variations

All six color variations apply to every lockup. Shown here on the primary horizontal lockup for reference.

All white

All black

Color accent on dark

Color accent on light

Full color on dark

Full color on light

Small size usage

At small sizes, the North Star element does not scale down legibly. Below the minimum size threshold, use the XS version which drops the star from the mark.

XS version

Use at sizes below 150px wide. The North Star element is removed for legibility.

1.5 inMinimum print size
150 pxMinimum screen size (standard)
Below 150 pxUse XS version

Do

  • Use on white or black backgrounds
  • Use approved brand colors when background allows
  • Maintain clear space around the mark
  • Scale proportionally at all times
  • Switch to XS version below 150px

Don't

  • Recreate or redraw any part of the logo
  • Add drop shadows or filter effects
  • Distort or change proportions
  • Place on complex images or unapproved colors
  • Rotate or place at an angle
  • Enclose within another shape
